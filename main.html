Information about Bunnies

- There are hundreds of bunny types in the world.
- Bunnies can be found in many places on Earth, like at homes or in forests.
- Bunnies can have different names because of their ears.
- A baby rabbit is called a Kit, a litter is how many kits a mother bunny has at one time.
