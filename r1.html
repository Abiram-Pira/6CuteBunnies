Netherland dwarf rabbit

Origin

The Netherlands

Apperance

Netherland dwarf rabbits are very small, have a big head, and short ears that stand up

Weight

1kg

Life Span

10 - 12 years.

Other

- Netherland dwarf rabbits make good pets.
- Their litter size is between 2 - 4 kits.
- Netherland dwarf rabbits are the smallest pet rabbits.

Polish rabbit

Origin

England

Apperance

Polish rabbits are very small, round, have large eyes, and ears that stick up.

Weight

Between 1 - 1.5kg

Life Span

5 - 6 years

Other

- Polish rabbits make good pets.
- They used to be eaten.
- Their litter size is between 2 - 4 kits.
- Likes attention.
