American fuzzy lop

Origin

The USA

Apperance

American fuzzy lops have long hair, short body, and their ears are floppy

Weight

1.4 - 1.8kg

Life Span

5 - 8 years.

Other

- American fuzzy lops make good pets.
- Their litter size is between 4 - 6 kits.

Holland lop

Origin

The Netherlands

Apperance

Holland lops are very small, have a short and wide, short and fat legs, and floppy ears

Weight

Between 0.9 - 1.8kg

Life Span

7 - 14 years

Other

- Polish rabbits make good pets.
- They are the smallest lops(bunnies with floppy ears)
- Their litter size is between 3 - 5 kits.
