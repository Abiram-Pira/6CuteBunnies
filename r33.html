Havana rabbit

Origin

The Netherlands

Apperance

Havana rabbits have a short head, short neck, a medium sized body, and ears that stick up.

Weight

2 - 2.9kg

Life Span

5 - 8 years.

Other

- Havana rabbits make good pets.
- Their litter size is between 6 - 9 kits. - they are playful and friendly.

Pygmy rabbit

Origin

The USA

Apperance

Pygmy rabbits are very small, have short ears, small back legs, and their ears stick up.

Weight

Between 350 - 500g

Life Span

3 - 5 years

Other

- Pygmy rabbits are wild animals.
- They are the smallest rabbits
- Their litter size is around 6 kits.
